Research Interests

My areas of research expertise are high-performance computing, numerical computation, and machine learning, with a particular focus on algorithm design and software development. My current research interests are centered around high-precision computing, rounding error analysis, mixed-precision techniques, and the application of deep reinforcement learning and federated learning methods.
